How to Add a Recipe

- Enter the recipe title and a brief description.
- Add ingredients by filling in the name, amount, and measurement, then click the "+" button.
- Add instructions by describing each step, then click the "+" button.
- Click "Save Recipe" to save your recipe.
- Use "Cancel" to discard changes.
- Use "Back" to return to the previous page.
- Use "Logout" to sign out of your account.
